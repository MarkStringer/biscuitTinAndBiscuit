About the Performance

We all have ideas. We all have dreams. Why is it so difficult for us to turn these ideas and dreams into reality? Mark Stringer has a degree in philosophy. He's a stand up comedian. He's also a project manager with fifteen years of experience of trying to turn ideas and dreams into reality . In this show he explains:

  • Why turning our ideas and dreams into reality is so hard.
  • Why just wanting something really badly and thinking about it really hard isn't enough.
  • What we can do to give our dreams and ideas the best chance of becoming a reality.
